About Us

History

California Goldens has been breeding Golden Retrievers since 1962. We are committed to the health and well-being of our dogs so we always put our dogs before everything else. It started as a small venture, but we are now fully committed to providing for our Goldens. Our initial location was in San Francisco but we have since moved to San Jose. Its been more than 50 years since we started, and it has now become a generational business. It brings us great joy to be able to breed our own puppies and match them up with loving families. Golden Retrievers are one of the most gentle breeds in the world.

What We Do

We have been helping families find their perfect furry companions for more than 50 years now. Our first priority is to ensure that our puppies' forever homes provide a safe and nurturing environment. We ensure that our puppies are up to date on their vaccinations and dewormers. They are also crate trained, which makes it easier for them to adapt to their new homes. Upon adoption, we provide free crates, toys, food, bowls, leash and collars. Goldens are one of the most intelligent and gentle breeds so they are extremely family and kid friendly.
